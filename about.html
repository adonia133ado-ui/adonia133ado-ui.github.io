About HiMicrowave

Engineering-driven RF & microwave component supplier serving global markets.

Who We Are

HiMicrowave is a specialized supplier of RF and microwave components, headquartered in Shenzhen, China. We bridge world-class Asian manufacturing with demanding applications in defense, telecommunications, test & measurement, and industrial markets worldwide.

Our portfolio spans RF amplifiers, filters, power combiners and dividers, waveguide components, and custom microwave assemblies — covering DC to 40 GHz and beyond.

What sets us apart is our engineering-led approach: we don't just supply parts, we help customers select the right components, provide application support, and ensure every product meets its datasheet specifications before shipment.
